NIL Compliance & Financial FAQ

Do NIL earnings count as taxable income?

Yes. NIL income must be reported on your tax return. We recommend setting aside 25–30% of your earnings for tax payments.

Can my school help manage my NIL deals?

Some schools offer compliance officers or legal advisors, but you are ultimately responsible for understanding your contracts and income.

What if I’m offered free products instead of cash?

Even non-cash NIL compensation (like gear or gifts) can be considered taxable income depending on its fair market value.
